Collaboration with other musicians

Home > Projects > Collaboration with other musicians

Alejandro Loguercio regularly plays in different projects together with other famous musicians.

For instance, he has performed as a solo violinist with the ensemble of Ksenija Sidorova, recording the "Carmen"-CD for "Deutsche Grammophon" in 2016. For more information please visit: www.ksenijasidorova.com

In addition, he often plays as a solo violinist in the Rojo-Tango-Ensemble of the star baritone, Erwin Schrott. For more information please see: www.erwinschrott.com
